MMP Glossary

MMP (Mobile Measurement Platform) +

Third-party attribution platform that measures and analyzes mobile app marketing performance across channels.

SKAN (SKAdNetwork) +

Apple's privacy-focused attribution framework for measuring iOS app install campaigns without user-level data.

Postbacks +

Server-to-server notifications sent by MMPs to ad networks when attribution events occur.

OneLink +

AppsFlyer's universal deep linking solution that routes users to the right app content across platforms.

ATT (App Tracking Transparency) +

iOS framework requiring user permission before tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies.

SRN/SAN +

Self-Reporting Networks / Self-Attributing Networks - ad platforms that report their own attribution data.
